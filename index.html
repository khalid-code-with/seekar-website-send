About Our Pedestal

Bridging Education & Industry Excellence.

Pedestal Techno World is a leading EdTech company committed to reducing the gap between education and industry. We provide cutting-edge training, real-world learning, and expert mentorship, empowering students with skills that drive success. Our mission is to transform learning into career opportunities through industry-aligned programs, hands-on experience, and strong placement support.

With a strong focus on practical learning and career development, we offer courses in Digital Marketing, Full-Stack Development, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and more. Our expert-led training, interactive platforms, and placement-driven approach ensure that learners gain the skills, confidence, and opportunities needed to thrive in today’s competitive job market. At Pedestal Techno World, we don’t just educate—we build careers.
